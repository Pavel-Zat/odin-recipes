Easy Meatloaf

Description

This is a very easy and no fail recipe for meatloaf. It won't take long to make at all, and it's quite good!

    Ingredients
  • 1 ½ pounds ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
    Steps
  1. In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, onion, milk and bread OR cracker crumbs. Season with salt and pepper to taste and place in a lightly greased 9x5-inch loaf pan, or form into a loaf and place in a lightly greased 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
